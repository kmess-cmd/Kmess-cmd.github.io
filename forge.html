// 01 · What I Make

A little of everything

No specialty, no limits. If you can dream it and it fits a printer bed, we can probably make it.

🎁
Gifts
Custom, personal, one-of-a-kind. The kind of thing you can't buy in a store.
🎨
Art & Decor
Sculptures, wall pieces, display items. Functional aesthetics or pure art.
🔧
Functional
Mounts, brackets, organizers, tools. Things that solve actual problems.
✨
Fun & Novelty
Weird, cool, unexpected. If it made you smile when you thought of it, that's enough.

Let's make something

Just ask.

No catalog, no minimums, no pressure. Tell me what you're thinking and I'll let you know if it's doable. I print things because I enjoy it — if I can help you out along the way, even better.

If you want to cover materials or just say thanks, there's a Buy Me a Coffee link below. Totally optional.

▸ Printer: Bambu A1
▸ Max size: 256 × 256 × 256mm (larger available)
▸ Location: Melbourne, FL
▸ Turnaround: Depends on complexity — I'll let you know
☕
Buy Me a Coffee

I print things because I enjoy it. But if I made something for you, or you just want to say thanks for the tool or blog post that helped you out — this is the place. No pressure, ever.
